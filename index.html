About Us

Kenya Vigen Homecare - Kenya is a registered NJ non-profit organization. Driven by the motive of upliftment of elderly care, Kenya Vigen Homecare - Kenya was established as a grassroot level non-profit organization. With a mission of building an inclusive, orphan & widow & widowers & elderly care sensitized society with equal access to essential healthcare , the foundation has been diligently working in 50 constituencies across 15 counties in Kenya. It is a registered entity under Foreign Contribution Regulation Act (FCRA) and officially associated with Widows & Widowers CBO - Kenya and other Departments of Government in Kenya. This organization serves as a centre for the elderly, orphans and patients with life limiting illness and those in need of assisted living at the comfort of their homes. who have been survivors of domestic violence and are in need of help in order to reform their lives and attain an identity of their own. Widows & Widowers CBO - Kenya promotes Gender Equality, and creates reformative opportunities for the physically handicapped via advancing cognizance among the underprivileged sections of the society. Having over 1 million beneficiaries already has brought recognition to the organization and its intensive efforts of community outreach over the years.

Our Mission : Embracing Every Life Journey

At Kenya Vigen Homecare, our mission transcends mere care. We weave a tapestry of compassion, dignity, and inclusion, where every thread represents an individual on their unique life journey.
Elderly: We provide a warm and welcoming haven, where seasoned wisdom finds solace, and physical limitations become stepping stones to continued joy and fulfillment. We celebrate their rich tapestry of experiences, honoring their voices and nurturing their spirits.
Physically Handicapped: We believe in exceeding limitations, not defining by them. We create a barrier-free environment where assistive technologies and unwavering support empower individuals to reach their full potential. We champion their strengths and celebrate their resilience.
Orphans: We offer a safe harbor, where childhood vulnerability finds a nurturing embrace. We provide a family of caring hearts, fostering love, security, and opportunities for growth. We empower them to dream, to learn, and to embrace a bright future.
Less Privileged: We extend a hand of solidarity, dismantling the walls of inequality. We provide access to essential healthcare, nutritious meals, and a network of support, fostering hope and self-sufficiency. We believe in building a community where no one is left behind.
Our mission is not just about care; it's about connection. We believe in fostering a vibrant tapestry of intergenerational relationships, where wisdom meets youthful exuberance, and compassion flows freely. We envision a world where every individual, regardless of age, ability, or circumstance, can find their place, their purpose, and their voice.
Join us today in weaving this tapestry of hope, dignity, and love. Together, we can create a world where every life journey is embraced, celebrated, and empowered.

A Tapestry of Ages: A Vision for Inclusive Care

In the heart of a bustling world, a haven emerges – not a fortress of isolation, but a vibrant tapestry woven from threads of silver, resilience, and youthful dreams. This is our vision: a home care unit where the elderly, the physically handicapped, and orphans find not just shelter, but a symphony of belonging
We envision a world without barriers, where assistive technologies become extensions of freedom, and ramps sing of accessibility.
Here, strength is redefined, not in spite of limitations, but because of them.

We see a world where every life journey, no matter how fragile or scarred, finds its place in the grand design.
